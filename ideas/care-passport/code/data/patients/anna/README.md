# Anna van der Berg

Anna is 74 years old and lives alone in her apartment in Utrecht. She was diagnosed with early-stage Alzheimer's-type dementia 18 months ago. Her husband Henk passed away three years ago. Her son Mark lives in Rotterdam — a 90-minute drive away. She receives home care visits twice daily from Buurtzorg.

Anna was a primary-school teacher for 35 years. She is proud, independent, and deeply private. She does not want to move to a care facility. She speaks Dutch only. Her cat Miep is her daily companion.
