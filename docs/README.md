## Working Product Frame

Build a WhatsApp voice companion that preserves a patient's voice over time, turns it into an evidence-backed care passport, and gives HCPs/caretakers a trustworthy interface for understanding needs, preferences, and care-gap risks.

The product supports human care decisions; it does not make legally or medically binding decisions.
