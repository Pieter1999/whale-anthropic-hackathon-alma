# Care preferences — Henk de Vries

## Bathing and personal care
- Showers, never baths. "Een matroos staat rechtop in het water." Do not run a bath even on bad days. {confidence: high, sources: [n_002#c_1], first_seen: 2026-04-05, last_confirmed: 2026-04-22}
- Wash from the feet up, not the head down — he says it's "verkeerd om" otherwise and will resist. {confidence: high, sources: [n_002#c_2], first_seen: 2026-04-05, last_confirmed: 2026-04-22}
- **Shaves himself with a Wilkinson Sword safety razor — do not offer to help.** If his hand is shaking, set out a hot wet flannel and step back. He will pause and resume. {confidence: high, sources: [n_006#c_7, n_008#c_3], first_seen: 2026-04-12, last_confirmed: 2026-04-23}
- Prefers male caregivers for intimate care. Has tolerated female caregivers post-stroke if introduced calmly and with the door left ajar. {confidence: medium, sources: [n_001#c_11, n_003#c_1], first_seen: 2026-04-02, last_confirmed: 2026-04-22}
- Approach from the left, always. Right ear is dead from forty years of engine-room. {confidence: high, sources: [n_003#c_5], first_seen: 2026-04-08, last_confirmed: 2026-04-22}
- Breton stripe shirts only — see personhood. Lay one out the night before; this prevents the morning standoff. {confidence: high, sources: [n_008#c_1], first_seen: 2026-04-12, last_confirmed: 2026-04-23, attributed: family/Saskia}

- **Small bottle of Beerenburg in the kitchen drawer, behind the tea towels — one finger before bed. Doctor knows.** Saskia stocks it.
