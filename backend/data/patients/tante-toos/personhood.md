# Personhood — Theodora "Tante Toos" Pengel

## Identity
- **Preferred name is "Tante Toos" or just "Tante"** — full name "Theodora Pengel" only on paperwork. Everyone has called her Tante for fifty years; she does not respond to "Mevrouw Pengel". {confidence: high, sources: [n_001#c_1, n_006#c_1], first_seen: 2026-04-01, last_confirmed: 2026-04-25}
- Born 1937 in Paramaribo, Surinam. Moved to the Netherlands in 1975, just after independence. Lived in Amsterdam Zuidoost ever since. {confidence: high, sources: [n_001#c_2, n_006#c_2], first_seen: 2026-04-01, last_confirmed: 2026-04-22}
- **Hospital cook at OLVG for 30 years (1978–2008)** — fed three generations of nurses. The nickname "Tante" started in that kitchen and never left her. {confidence: high, sources: [n_002#c_1, n_007#c_1], first_seen: 2026-04-05, last_confirmed: 2026-04-23}
- Lives with her son Edgar and his wife Soraya in a Bijlmer flat. Three rooms; her room faces the gallery. {confidence: high, sources: [n_001#c_3, n_003#c_1], first_seen: 2026-04-01, last_confirmed: 2026-04-25}

- Bilingual Dutch and Sranantongo. **Drifts to Sranantongo when tired, upset, or talking about food.** Soraya speaks both. Edgar understands more than he speaks. {confidence: high, sources: [n_002#c_2, n_006#c_3], first_seen: 2026-04-05, last_confirmed: 2026-04-25}

## Language and communication
